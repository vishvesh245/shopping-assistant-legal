Privacy Policy: AI Shopping Assistant for noon

Last updated: February 23, 2026

What This Extension Does

The AI Shopping Assistant is a Chrome extension designed to enhance your experience on noon.com. It uses AI-powered conversation to help users find products, compare prices, and discover deals in real-time.

Data Collection and Privacy

We prioritize your privacy. The AI Shopping Assistant does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require account creation, and we do not track your identity.

Chat Messages & AI Processing

When you interact with the assistant:

  • Your messages are sent to a secure backend server to be processed by a Large Language Model (LLM) API (such as OpenAI or Gemini) to generate shopping advice.
  • Messages are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers.
  • Data shared with the AI is limited to the text of your query and relevant product metadata from the page you are viewing to ensure accurate recommendations.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's storage API solely to:

  • Save your chat history locally on your device so you don't lose your conversation if you refresh the page.
  • Remember your preferences, such as language or search filters.

This data remains on your local machine and is never uploaded to our servers.

Website Access

The extension only activates on noon.com domains. It does not access, read, or modify data on any other websites you visit.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide the assistant's core functionality:

  • LLM API Provider: To process natural language and provide shopping recommendations. No personal data is intentionally shared with these providers.

Data Sharing & Analytics

  • No Data Selling: We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.
  • No Tracking: We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking pixels to monitor your browsing behavior.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices. Any updates will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
